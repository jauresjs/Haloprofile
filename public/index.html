Frequently Asked Questions

What kind of photos should I upload?

For the best results, upload 15-20 clear photos of your face. Use a mix of angles, expressions, and lighting. Avoid group shots, sunglasses, or heavy filters.

Are the generated photos realistic?

Yes! Our custom AI is trained specifically to retain your natural facial features, skin texture, and body type. It looks just like you, on your very best day.

What happens to my uploaded photos?

Your privacy is our priority. Your original photos are used solely to train your personal AI model and are securely processed. We do not sell your data.

Can I cancel my subscription?

Absolutely. You can cancel your subscription at any time directly from your dashboard. Your unused credits will remain valid until the end of your billing cycle.
